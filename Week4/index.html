About Lisbon

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a city steeped in history and culture. Known for its vibrant neighborhoods like Alfama and Bairro Alto, Lisbon offers a mix of old-world charm and modernity. The city is famous for its iconic yellow trams, scenic viewpoints called "miradouros," and delicious culinary offerings such as "Pastel de Nata." Among its most popular tourist attractions are the historic Belém Tower, the sprawling Jerónimos Monastery, and the modern MAAT museum. Nearby, the enchanting town of Sintra, with its fairytale-like castles and lush landscapes, is a must-visit. Lisbon's welcoming vibe and picturesque streets make it a destination to remember.
